Task 1

Ubiquitous

Technology is all around us and is a part of our everyday life at any time. From an alarm waking you up, to communicate with others, entertainment, automating daily tasks… There is no area in our life that is not affected and/or enhanced by technology.

Enabler

Technology is not a solution in itself. It facilitates solving problems and perform actions. A medium that makes possible finding new ways of expression like video-blogging, assist with translation between languages to communicate, use voice to complete tasks, help us navigate around the world...

Democratiser

Technology provides equal access and opportunities to people that otherwise would not be able to. From real-time access to the latest information, new ways of education… Everyone with access to technology, knowledgeable of it and with connection to Internet could reach the same level of opportunities.

Ethical implications

The standardization of technology brings questions about its ethical implications. Are the machine learning algorithms designed with bias? Is the data collected in an ethical way, how is that dataset used? Is our personal privacy affected by the owners of the technology?...

5G

Lightspeed connection

The latest mobile network generation expected to be up to 100x faster than 4G. Referred at the network of networks. 5G will operate lower response times, lower latency and with higher network capacity.

  • Quicker and wider access to knowledge
  • Unlock the connection of the next billion of users
  • Accelerate adoption of IoT, Machine Learning and other technologies
  • Uncover new use cases and innovation on every sector

Sensory computing

A network of devices that collects and exchanges data generated through wearables, appliances and agents existing in “smart” cities. Connected thanks to the use of smartphones. With IoT, devices will connect autonomously expanding our connected society.

  • Creates new autonomous ways of interaction that requires no human intervention
  • Creates more data points to capture, measure, analyse and act on with digital tools
  • Accelerates automation of tasks traditionally performed by humans
  • Cheap and easy access to IoT devices foster adoption and the creation of a community of makers

Automated learning

The field of study of AI where computing systems learn from data sets, identify patterns , act and/or make decisions with different levels of human intervention. Analytical models and algorithms to help computers find patterns and make predictions.

  • Understand data generated and be able to predict future information
  • Enable task automation performed by robots and autonomous entities
  • Provide intelligence to computing resources
  • AI outperforms human capacity (e.g. eliminating functions as sleep or eating)

new technology

Maximising connectivity

Cloud computing is a network practice to store, manage and access data from remote servers instead of a local network Edge computing evolves Cloud computing by offering computing resources to users. These resources are closer to where data is generated, at the edge of the network. The data will travel shorter distances and be more secure.

  • Reduces overall usage of current computing resources
  • Better performance for complex data analysis and machine learning algorithms
  • Improves data transmission and connectivity globally

Microservices and containers

Microservices is a modern type of software architecture where independent services perform specific functions of an application. Containers are software packages where the core of an application is bundled with its dependencies to run quickly and reliably in any environment.

Code structure

App.js is the main loop component that works similarly as in game development, where there is a state machine to render the relevant UI sections:

  • Splash, to display on startup (didn’t had time to manually build into the project)

  • Intro, the onboarding screens. This has a Swiper component handling the interaction and a Button component to display in each.

  • App, the AR section with the marker. Includes ViroARSceneNavigator which handles all scenes and within it there is a ViroARScene object, called MainSceneAR:

    • ViroARScene renders the main scene
    • All 3D nodes should be included in a ViroNode
    • All 2D nodes are included in ViroFlexView

solution approach

The technology behind

  • Android technology for mobile devices will be the main target for the Product development
  • Microservices will be key to Scale the solution in terms of feature development and user base. This will help also in outage moments, so they are always up and running
  • Alliance with Telcos to leverage a 5G experience. Standard Bank could partner with a Telco to provide a better Internet experience with their customers.
  • Embrace the power of social media and messaging tools as Whatsapp for customer service support (considering security and data protection implications)
  • Appointment booking tools integrated with SB systems for face to face and over the phone interactions
  • Ongoing notifications and alerts to notify users about financial updates
  • Adoption of Game mechanics to find new ways to engage users with financial content so they learn by doing

solution approach

Leveraging Standard Bank

  • To build trust on the institution, it should provide transparency, communicate upfront the risk and implications of financial decisions
  • Plan the transition of current customers to the new solution, not focusing only on new users
  • Be approachable and available easily to customers
  • Standard Bank should align strengths with customer needs
  • Present educational content to adapt basic concepts on finance and digital to their daily lives
  • Increase the power of the community and word of mouth within their digital offering to support and incentive your users and increase brand perception
  • Invest in providing community workshops to improve finance and awareness of finance status
  • Validate the concept with a small set of users, scale the solution within a country and then translate the model to another market
